<p><ul><li>Flanged Connection</li><li>Threaded Connection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ball check valves market can be primarily categorized into two connection types: flanged connections and threaded connections. Flanged connection valves feature flat surfaces with holes for bolting, allowing for easy installation and removal, ideal for larger pipelines. In contrast, threaded connection valves utilize screw threads for compact installations, suitable for smaller pipes and systems where space is limited. Each type serves specific applications in industries like water treatment, oil and gas, and HVAC, ensuring efficient flow control and preventing backflow.</p></p>
