<p><p>The flange bolts market is divided into two primary types: partial thread and full thread bolts. In contrast, full thread flange bolts are fully threaded along their entire length, offering maximum engagement and strength, suitable for applications requiring robust fastening solutions. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs and installation environments.</p></p>
