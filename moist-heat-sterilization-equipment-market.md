<p><strong>The Moist Heat Sterilization Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Steam Sterilization</li><li>Steam-air Mixture Sterilization</li><li>Steam-water Mixture Sterilization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Moist heat sterilization equipment encompasses various types, primarily steam sterilization, steam-air mixture sterilization, and steam-water mixture sterilization. Steam sterilization employs saturated steam under pressure to eliminate microorganisms, ensuring effective sterilization in medical and laboratory settings. Steam-air mixture sterilization combines steam with air for slow penetration, suitable for heat-sensitive materials. Steam-water mixture sterilization incorporates water to generate a consistent steam environment, enhancing heat transfer for sterilization. Together, these methods provide reliable options for maintaining hygiene and safety in healthcare.</p></p>
