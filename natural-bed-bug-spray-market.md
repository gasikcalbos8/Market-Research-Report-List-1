<p><ul><li>Contact Sprays</li><li>Residual Spray</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The natural bed bug spray market can be categorized into two main types: contact sprays and residual sprays. Contact sprays are designed to eliminate bed bugs on direct contact, providing immediate results by targeting pests that are present at the time of application. In contrast, residual sprays leave a lasting protective barrier, killing bed bugs over an extended period as they come into contact with treated surfaces. Both types offer eco-friendly alternatives to conventional pesticides, appealing to consumers seeking safer pest control solutions.</p></p>
