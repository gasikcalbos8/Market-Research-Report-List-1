<p><strong>The District Cooling Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Free Cooling</li><li>Absorption Cooling</li><li>Electric Chillers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>District Cooling Systems (DCS) operate through various technologies for efficient temperature control. Free Cooling utilizes ambient air or water to cool systems, reducing energy consumption by harnessing natural resources. Absorption Cooling employs heat sources, like steam or hot water, to drive a refrigeration cycle, making it ideal for waste heat recovery. Electric Chillers, commonly used in DCS, convert electrical energy into thermal energy for cooling, providing flexibility and reliability. Each type enhances energy efficiency and sustainability in urban cooling applications.</p></p>
