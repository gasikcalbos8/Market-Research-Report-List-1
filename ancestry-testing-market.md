<p><strong>The Ancestry Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Y chromosome testing</li><li>Mitochondrial DNA testing</li><li>Single nucleotide polymorphism testing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ancestry testing encompasses various market types, including Y chromosome testing, mitochondrial DNA testing, and single nucleotide polymorphism (SNP) testing. Y chromosome testing is exclusive to males and traces paternal lineage through paternal lines. Mitochondrial DNA testing, available to all, follows maternal ancestry through the maternal line. SNP testing analyzes specific genetic variations across individuals, providing insights into diverse ancestral origins. Together, these testing methods cater to different interests, helping individuals uncover their heritage and ancestral connections.</p></p>
