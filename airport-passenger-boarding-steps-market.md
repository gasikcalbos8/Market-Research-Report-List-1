<p><p>Airport passenger boarding steps are essential equipment used to facilitate the boarding and deboarding of passengers onto aircraft. The market can be divided into two main types: towable and self-propelled. Towable types are trailers pulled by ground vehicles, offering flexibility and cost-effectiveness for airports with varying aircraft sizes. In contrast, self-propelled types are motorized units that can operate independently, providing faster service and better maneuverability. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing airport efficiency and passenger convenience.</p></p>
