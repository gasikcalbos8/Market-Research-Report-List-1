<p><p>The skin rejuvenation devices market comprises various technologies that enhance skin appearance. Laser devices utilize focused light for pigmentation and wrinkle removal. Radiofrequency devices promote collagen production through heating tissues. Intense Pulsed Light (IPL) devices target discoloration and vascular lesions with broad-spectrum light. Mechanical energy devices, like microneedles, stimulate skin through micro-injuries. LED devices employ light wavelengths to address acne and skin texture. Other devices may include ultrasound or cryotherapy, each offering unique benefits for skin rejuvenation.</p></p>
