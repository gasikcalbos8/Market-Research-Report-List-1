<p><p>The two-wheeler position sensor market encompasses various technologies used to determine the position of components in motorcycles and scooters. Capacitive sensors detect changes in capacitance to ascertain position, while magnetic sensors utilize magnetic fields for precise tracking. Infrared (IR) sensors emit light to measure distances accurately, and force sensors gauge pressure to ensure optimal positioning. Other types may include inductive or ultrasonic sensors, each contributing to enhanced safety, performance, and efficiency in two-wheeler applications, catering to evolving consumer needs.</p></p>
