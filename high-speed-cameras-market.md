The 5,001 to 20,000 fps range is ideal for more detailed analysis in scientific and industrial settings. Cameras capturing 20,001 to 100,000 fps are used in advanced research, while those exceeding 100,000 fps are designed for specialized applications like ballistics and high-speed phenomena studies.</p></p>
