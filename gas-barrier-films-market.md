<p><p>Gas barrier films are specialized materials designed to restrict the permeation of gases, moisture, and volatile compounds. Key types include PET (polyethylene terephthalate), known for its strength and clarity; CPP (cast polypropylene), valued for flexibility; BOPP (biaxially oriented polypropylene), ideal for packaging; EVOH (ethylene vinyl alcohol), known for excellent barrier properties; PA (polyamide), offering robustness; and PVDC (polyvinylidene chloride), recognized for moisture resistance. Other films may include unique blends or coatings tailored for specific applications in food packaging, pharmaceuticals, and industrial uses.</p></p>
