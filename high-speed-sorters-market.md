<p><p>High-speed sorters are essential in logistics and warehousing for efficient package handling. The market features two primary types: case sorters and unit sorters. Case sorters are designed to handle bulk items, categorizing and directing them based on specific destinations. In contrast, unit sorters focus on individual items or small packages, enhancing accuracy and speed in sorting. Both systems optimize operations, reduce labor costs, and improve delivery timelines, making them vital for businesses in e-commerce and supply chain management.</p></p>
