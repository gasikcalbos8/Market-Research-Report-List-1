<p><p>The Fine Dust Sensor market is categorized into two main types based on their output capabilities: sensors that measure PM10 and those that measure PM2.5. PM10 sensors track particulate matter with a diameter of 10 micrometers or less, capturing larger dust particles that can affect respiratory health. In contrast, PM2.5 sensors focus on smaller particles, 2.5 micrometers or less, which can penetrate deeper into the lungs and pose more significant health risks. Both types are vital for air quality monitoring and pollution control.</p></p>
