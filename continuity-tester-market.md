<p><p>The continuity tester market encompasses several specific types, each designed for distinct applications. Ground continuity testers ensure proper grounding in electrical systems. Voltage and continuity testers check for electrical voltage and circuit continuity. Digital insulation and continuity testers assess insulation resistance alongside circuit continuity. Hose continuity testers are used in hydraulic applications to verify the electrical continuity of hoses. Circuit continuity testers primarily detect broken connections in various electronic circuits, enhancing safety and functionality in electrical systems.</p></p>
