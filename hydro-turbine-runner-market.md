<p><p>The hydro turbine runner market comprises several key types: Francis, Kaplan, Pelton, and others. Francis turbines are versatile and used for medium head applications, while Kaplan turbines are optimal for low head sites with variable water flows. Pelton turbines excel in high head environments, utilizing impulse energy. Other turbine types may include Crossflow and Bulb turbines, each designed for specific hydraulic conditions. This diversity allows hydroelectric projects to maximize efficiency and energy production across various geographical and operational contexts.</p></p>
