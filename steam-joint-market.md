<p><strong>The Steam Joint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rotary steam joint</li><li>Non-rotating steam joint</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steam joint market is divided into two primary types: rotary steam joints and non-rotating steam joints. Rotary steam joints facilitate the continuous rotation of machinery while allowing steam transfer, ideal for applications requiring movement, such as in turbines and rotary molds. In contrast, non-rotating steam joints connect stationary components, ensuring a secure steam seal without movement, suited for fixed installations. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, impacting efficiency and performance in steam-based systems.</p></p>
