<p><p>The magnetic proximity sensor market includes two primary types: fixed distance sensors and adjustable distance sensors. Fixed distance sensors detect magnetic fields at predetermined distances, making them ideal for applications requiring consistent detection points. In contrast, adjustable distance sensors provide flexibility, allowing users to modify the detection range based on specific needs. This adaptability enhances their utility across various industries, such as automotive, industrial automation, and security, catering to diverse operational requirements while ensuring reliable performance.</p></p>
