<p><p>The wafer cutting blades market is categorized into hub dicing blades and hubless dicing blades. Hub dicing blades feature a central hub that enhances stability and precision during cutting, making them suitable for high-volume production. In contrast, hubless dicing blades are designed without a central hub, allowing for reduced kerf loss and improved flexibility in cutting intricate patterns. Both types serve critical roles in semiconductor manufacturing, catering to varying needs in efficiency, precision, and specific application requirements.</p></p>
