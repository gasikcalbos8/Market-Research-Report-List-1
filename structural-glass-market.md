<p><p>The structural glass market is primarily categorized into frame type and frameless type. The frame type incorporates a supportive framework, typically made of metals or other materials, which enhances stability and durability, making it suitable for various architectural applications. In contrast, the frameless type features glass panels that are unsupported, creating a sleek, minimalist aesthetic that maximizes visibility and light flow. Both types are favored for modern construction due to their aesthetic appeal and ability to create expansive, open spaces.</p></p>
