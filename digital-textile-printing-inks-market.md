<p><strong>The Digital Textile Printing Inks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dispersed Inks</li><li>Reactive Inks</li><li>Acid Inks</li><li>Pigment Inks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital textile printing inks encompass various types tailored for specific fabrics and applications. Dispersed inks are primarily used for polyester and synthetic fibers, offering vibrant colors through thermal transfer. Reactive inks bond chemically with fibers, ideal for natural fabrics like cotton, achieving bright, long-lasting prints. Acid inks are designed for silk and nylon, providing excellent color vibrancy and ease of use. Pigment inks are versatile, working on multiple substrates, and are appreciated for their durability and eco-friendliness.</p></p>
