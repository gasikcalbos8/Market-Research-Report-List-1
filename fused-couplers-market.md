<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Fused Couplers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Mode Couplers</li><li>Multi-mode Couplers</li><li>Polarization Maintaining (PM) Couplers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fused couplers are essential components in fiber optic systems, primarily categorized into three types. Single mode couplers facilitate transmission through a single light path, ideal for long-distance communication with minimal loss. Multi-mode couplers support multiple light paths, enhancing bandwidth over shorter distances. Polarization maintaining (PM) couplers preserve the polarization state of light, making them valuable in sensitive applications where signal integrity is crucial. Each type serves distinct needs, catering to various sectors within the fiber optic market.</p></p>
