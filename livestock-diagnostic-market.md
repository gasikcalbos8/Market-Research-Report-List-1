<p><p>The Livestock Diagnostic Market is segmented into three main types: immunodiagnosis, molecular diagnosis, and other technologies. Immunodiagnosis involves tests that detect antibodies or antigens in blood, aiding in the identification of diseases like brucellosis or bovine tuberculosis. Molecular diagnosis utilizes techniques such as PCR to identify pathogens at the genetic level, allowing for rapid and precise detection of diseases. Other technologies encompass various emerging methods, including PCR-based assays and advanced imaging techniques, expanding diagnostic capabilities for livestock health management.</p></p>
