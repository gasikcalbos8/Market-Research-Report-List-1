<p><p>The spotting scopes market is primarily divided into two types: straight body and angled body spotting scopes. Straight body scopes feature a linear design that aligns with the user's line of sight, making them ideal for quick target acquisition in open areas. Angled body scopes, on the other hand, have an eyepiece positioned at an angle, providing more comfortable viewing at higher angles and ease of use for extended periods. Both types cater to different user preferences and specific outdoor activities.</p></p>
