<p><p>Wafer processing and assembly equipment encompasses various technologies vital for semiconductor fabrication. Chemical Mechanical Polishing (CMP) equipment ensures uniform surface flatness, essential for subsequent layers. Etching equipment selectively removes material to create intricate patterns. Thin film deposition equipment applies uniform layers of materials for device functionality. Photoresist processing equipment prepares substrates for lithography by applying photosensitive layers. Assembly equipment facilitates the assembly of semiconductor devices, integrating various components into a cohesive unit for functionality in electronic applications.</p></p>
