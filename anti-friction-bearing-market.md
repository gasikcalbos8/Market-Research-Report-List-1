<p><p>The anti-friction bearing market includes three main types: ball bearings, roller bearings, and other bearings. Ball bearings use spherical balls to reduce friction, offering high speed and low friction, ideal for light-duty applications. Roller bearings employ cylindrical rollers, providing increased load capacity and stability, suitable for heavier loads. Other bearings encompass specialized designs, such as needle or thrust bearings, catering to specific requirements in various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing, enhancing performance and efficiency in machinery.</p></p>
