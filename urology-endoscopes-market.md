Rigid urology endoscopes are designed for procedures requiring precision and stability, often used in surgeries like cystoscopy and ureteroscopy. They provide clear visualization but have limited maneuverability. In contrast, flexible urology endoscopes offer enhanced adaptability, allowing greater access to complex anatomical structures.
