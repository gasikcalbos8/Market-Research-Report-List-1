<p><ul><li>Wheeled Type</li><li>Crawler Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tiller machinery is categorized into two main market types: wheeled and crawler. Wheeled tillers are designed for mobility and ease of operation on flat, solid terrain, making them ideal for small to medium-sized farms. They offer quicker transportation between fields. In contrast, crawler tillers feature a tracked design that provides better stability and traction on uneven or muddy surfaces, making them suited for larger agricultural areas or rough terrains. Each type serves distinct farming needs based on land conditions.</p></p>
