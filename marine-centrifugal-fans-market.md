<p><p>Marine centrifugal fans are essential for ventilation in marine applications, and they generally come in two main types: direct drive and belt drive. </p><p>Direct drive fans are mechanically connected to the motor, offering higher efficiency and reduced maintenance due to fewer moving parts. In contrast, belt drive fans use a pulley system, allowing for more flexibility in speed adjustments and greater noise isolation. Each type serves distinct needs in marine environments, balancing efficiency, maintenance, and performance based on specific operational requirements.</p></p>
