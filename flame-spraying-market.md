<p><ul><li>Wire Flame Spraying</li><li>Powder Flame Spraying</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flame spraying is a thermal spray coating process that involves the application of materials to surfaces for protection or enhancement. There are two primary types: wire flame spraying, which uses metal wires that are melted and atomized to produce coatings, and powder flame spraying, where powdered materials are fed into a flame and sprayed onto surfaces. Each method offers distinct advantages, with wire spraying providing a smoother finish and powder spraying allowing for a wider range of material options and properties.</p></p>
