<p><p>The solar inverters market comprises two primary types: the Independent Model and the Integrated Model. The Independent Model refers to standalone inverter systems that are separate from the solar panels, allowing for flexibility in installation and upgrades. Conversely, the Integrated Model combines inverters within the solar panel systems, streamlining installation and reducing space requirements. Both models cater to different consumer needs, with the Independent Model offering customization and the Integrated Model providing convenience and efficiency in solar energy management.</p></p>
