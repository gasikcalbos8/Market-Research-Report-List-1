<p><p>The bioresorbable collagen implant market comprises three primary types: collagen sponges, collagen gels/hydrogels, and collagen pellets. Collagen sponges serve as 3D scaffolds for tissue regeneration, promoting cell infiltration and healing. Collagen gels or hydrogels provide a moist environment, enhancing drug delivery and cellular interactions. Collagen pellets are compact forms that offer localized delivery of growth factors, facilitating targeted healing. Together, these types address various medical needs, supporting tissue repair and regeneration in diverse clinical applications.</p></p>
