<p><p>The Zinc Oxide Active Market encompasses three primary types of production methods. The Direct Process, also known as the America Method, involves heating zinc in excess air to produce zinc oxide directly. The Indirect Process, or French Method, derives zinc oxide from a two-step process, first producing zinc metal from ores followed by oxidation. The Wet Chemical Process involves dissolving zinc salts in solutions to precipitate zinc oxide, offering a more controlled and environmentally friendly approach to production.</p></p>
