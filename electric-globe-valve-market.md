<p><strong>The Electric Globe Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Straight-Through Globe Valve</li><li>Straight Flow Globe Valve</li><li>Angle Type Globe Valve</li><li>Plunger Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric globe valve market comprises various types tailored for specific applications. The straight-through globe valve allows fluid flow in a straight path, reducing pressure loss. The straight flow globe valve offers minimal turbulence, enhancing flow efficiency. The angle type globe valve diverts flow at an angle, ideal for space-restricted installations. Lastly, the plunger valve features a plunger mechanism for precision control of flow, making it suitable for throttling and regulating applications. Each type serves distinct operational needs in fluid management systems.</p></p>
