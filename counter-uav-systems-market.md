<p><p>Counter-UAV systems can be categorized into three main types: ground-based, air-based, and handheld. Air-based systems involve drone-mounted technologies that intercept or disable unauthorized UAVs from the air. Handheld systems are portable devices operated by personnel, allowing for quick deployment and targeting of drones in specific areas. Each type serves unique operational needs and environments in countering unauthorized UAV activities.</p></p>
