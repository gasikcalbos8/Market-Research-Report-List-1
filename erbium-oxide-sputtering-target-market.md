<p><p>The Erbium Oxide Sputtering Target market is categorized into two main types: Rotatable Transformation and Non-Rotating Type. Rotatable Transformation targets allow for continuous material deposition by rotating during the sputtering process, enhancing uniformity and efficiency. In contrast, Non-Rotating Type targets remain static, typically used for specific applications where uniformity is less critical. Each type serves distinct needs in thin-film deposition processes, with varying impacts on production speed, material utilization, and overall coating quality in semiconductor and optical industries.</p></p>
