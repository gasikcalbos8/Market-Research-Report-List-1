<p><ul><li>Nickel-cadmium Battery</li><li>NiMH Battery</li><li>Lithium Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PC battery market comprises three main types: Nickel-Cadmium (NiCd), Nickel-Metal Hydride (NiMH), and Lithium-ion (Li-ion). NiCd batteries are known for their durability and resistance to extreme temperatures, but they suffer from memory effect and environmental concerns. NiMH batteries provide higher capacity and are more eco-friendly than NiCd. Li-ion batteries dominate due to their lightweight design, higher energy density, and longer lifespan, making them the preferred choice for modern laptops and mobile devices.</p></p>
