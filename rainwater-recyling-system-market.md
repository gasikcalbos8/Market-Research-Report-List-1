<p><p>The rainwater recycling system market can be categorized into three main types: direct-pumped, gravity, and others. Direct-pumped systems utilize electric or mechanical pumps to transport harvested rainwater for various uses, ensuring accessibility regardless of tank placement. Gravity systems rely on natural gravitational flow, reducing energy costs and maintenance, as water is distributed based on tank elevation. Other types may include hybrid systems or those incorporating advanced filtration and treatment technologies, further enhancing water quality and usability across different applications.</p></p>
